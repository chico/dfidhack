We all buy everyday goods but do you know what impact trade barriers and trade-related costs have on prices?
Trademize brings trade transparency to you through eye-catching reports and interactive tools.

 

Trade Principles

Trade Tariff Simple average applied tariffs are a tax on import that act as trade barriers. The simple average applied tariff in India on shoes was 52% in 1996.
Customs and other import duties Customs and other import duties are all levies collected on goods that are entering the country or services delivered by nonresidents to residents. They include levies imposed for revenue or protection purposes and determined on a specific or ad valorem basis as long as they are restricted to imported goods or services.
Cost to import Cost measures the fees levied on a 20-foot container in U.S. dollars. All the fees associated with completing the procedures to export or import the goods are included. The cost measure does not include tariffs or trade taxes.

Major trade products

Bottled Water The simple average applied tariff in Nigeria on bottled water was 60% in 2000.
Black Tea The simple average applied tariff in India on black tea has been 100% in the past 5 years.
Shoes The simple average applied tariff in South Africa on shoes has been 30% in the past 12 years.
